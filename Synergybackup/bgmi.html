BGMI (Battlegrounds Showdown)-2023

The Battleground Showdown, a BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) Tournament, was successfully conducted in collaboration with Esya’23, IIITD’s Annual Tech Fest, and Synergy, IGDTUW’s Sports Council. This prestigious event garnered significant attention, with an online presence of more than 5000+ people. The tournament brought together 400+ players and 96 teams who engaged in fierce competition to claim the coveted title of the ultimate champion.✨
MODE : ONLINE
DATES : August, 5 2023 - August, 8 2023

Registerations Closed



Rules


1.Group Stage : To ensure a fair and competitive environment, the participating teams were initially divided into 4 groups, each comprising 24 teams. In this stage, teams played 3 games on each of the three major maps (Erangel, Miramar, and Sanhok). The top 10 teams from each group advanced to the semi-finals.
2.Semi finals : Following the group stage, the top 40 teams progressed to the semi-finals. These 40 teams were divided into 2 groups, each containing 20 teams. In this stage, they battled it out in 6 intense games per group. From each group, the top 8 teams secured their place in the finals.
3.Finals : The pinnacle of the Battleground Showdown unfolded over a two-day period. The top 16 teams, as determined by the semi-finals, faced off in an electrifying series of games to determine the ultimate champions of the tournament.
